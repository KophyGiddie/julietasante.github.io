About Me

ABOUT ME

Juliet Asante is an award winning Film Maker, Entrepreneur and Activist. Her awards include the ‘Hollywood-Reporter’s’ next generation awards -2009. The ʻWorld of a difference 100ʼ by the International Alliance for Women (TIAW)- 2009 and the ‘The Best Actress Award’. She is the founder of Eagle Productions ltd, a Media Production, Communications and Consulting company and Save Our Women Int. that focuses on girl education and health. Her experience and network across the continent of Africa, makes her a much sort after consultant and partner for both international and local interests. Juliet has spent a lot of time helping to develop economic opportunity for women and was the founder and president of the Eagle Women Empowerment club, impacting women businesses across the continent of Africa. She served on the World Economic Council’s Global Agenda Council on Entertainment and has written and directed some of the longest running shows on Ghana Television. Juliet is an avid blogger for the Huffington Post, an Aspen global leadership fellow, a Legatum Institute fellow, an MIT legatum fellow and mentor. She is the innovator of Mobilefliks. The first of its kind on the continent of Africa, Mobilefliks are short movies made for the mobile phones. By working with the Networks and exploring developing distribution platforms, Mobilefliks is revolutionizing the way film is made, distributed and consumed on the continent. She continues to mentor, help companies develop and explore opportunities in Africa, speak on entrepreneurship and consult in media and communications. She remains committed her first love, Film making.
